Social Media - Effects

The website portrays the effects of social media on the student's life in following aspects:

  • Physical
  • Mental
  • Academics
  • Social

Note: The effects are further disussed detailed in next section!

Students using social media

Advantages

Access to Educational Resources

~ Students can access tutorials, research articles, and learning videos through platforms like YouTube and Khan Academy.

Collaboration and Communication

~ Platforms such as WhatsApp and Facebook allow students to communicate with each other

Improved Digital Literacy

~ Using social media helps students develop research skills

Networking Opportunities

~ Students can connect with: Professionals, Mentors and Educational communities

Disadvantages

Distraction

~ Constant notifications and scrolling reduce concentration during study time.

Poor Time Management

~ Excessive social media use can reduce study hours leading to academic losses

Sleep Disturbance

~ Late-night browsing affects sleep quality, leading to Fatigue and reduced focus

Mental Health Issues

~ Comparison, cyberbullying, and online pressure may cause Anxiety, Depression and Low self-esteem
